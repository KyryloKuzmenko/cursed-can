Experience the Unique Thrills of Cursed Can

  • Scatter Wins:

    Wins from Cursed Can symbols are multiplied by your total bet and added to payline wins.

  • The Key: Cursed Can Symbol:

    This special symbol triggers the Free Spins bonus and plays a crucial role in the Spooky Spins feature.

  • Classic 5-Reel, 3-Row Slot:

    Enjoy a familiar and engaging slot layout with 20 fixed paylines for maximum winning opportunities.

  • Unlock the Free Spins:

    Land 3 or more Cursed Can symbols anywhere on the reels to activate 10 Free Spins!

  • Maximum Free Spins:

    You can play a maximum of 40 Free Spins in a single game, with extra Free Spins being won a maximum of 3 times.

  • Rewarding Cursed Can:

    Even after transforming Crops, the Cursed Can symbol can still award extra Free Spins during the bonus round.

  • Wild Symbol Power:

    Wilds appear on any reel in both the main game and Free Spins, substituting for all other symbols except the Cursed Can. Land 5 Wilds for the highest possible win.

  • Random Event Surprise:

    In both the main game and Free Spins, a Random Event can highlight 1-3 reels, filling them entirely with a random high-paying symbol for a guaranteed payout! Transformed Crop Wilds can also appear during this event.

  • Spooky Spins Transformation:

    During Free Spins, high-paying Crop symbols have a droplet meter. Each Cursed Can symbol landed removes a droplet from the cheapest high-paying Crop. Once the meter is empty, that Crop symbol turns Wild for the remainder of the Free Spins!
