Your Guide to Growing Wins in Cursed Can

  • Set your desired bet amount for the 20 fixed paylines.

  • Spin the 5 colorful reels and anticipate forming winning combinations from left to right.

  • The Wild symbol will substitute for most other symbols (excluding the Cursed Can) to help you create winning lines.

  • Land 3 or more Cursed Can symbols anywhere on the reels to trigger the exciting Free Spins bonus round with 10 free spins.

  • During Free Spins, watch the droplet meters on the high-paying Crop symbols. Landing Cursed Can symbols will reduce these meters, eventually transforming Crops into Wild symbols.

  • Be on the lookout for the Random Event, where highlighted reels can instantly award wins with high-paying symbols.

  • Wins from the Cursed Can symbol are multiplied by your total bet and added to any payline wins.

  • Aim to land more Cursed Can symbols during Free Spins to re-trigger additional free spins, up to a maximum of 40 in total.

  • Remember that Crop symbols transformed into Wilds will remain Wild for the entire duration of the Free Spins bonus.

  • For detailed information on symbol payouts and game rules, please consult the Paytable within the game.
