Witness Symbol Transformations and Unexpected Rewards!

Get ready for two captivating features that set Cursed Can apart!

  • Spooky Spins:

    During the Free Spins bonus, observe the droplet meters on the high-paying Crop symbols. Every Cursed Can symbol that appears will remove a droplet from the cheapest high-paying Crop. Once a Crop's meter reaches zero, it will magically transform into a Wild symbol for the rest of your Free Spins, potentially leading to significant wins!

  • Random Event:

    At any point during the main game or Free Spins, a thrilling Random Event can occur! One, two, or three reels will be randomly selected and highlighted, then filled with a random high-paying symbol, guaranteeing a payout. If a Crop symbol has already transformed into a Wild during Free Spins, it can also appear as a Wild during this exciting event!
